Hydrogen VS Electric Cars

Will hydrogen-fueled cars ever catch up to EVs?

The debate between hydrogen and electric vehicles is intensifying as the world seeks more sustainable alternatives to fossil fuels. While electric vehicles (EVs) have dominated the market in recent years, with an expanding charging infrastructure and decreasing battery costs, hydrogen fuel cell vehicles (FCEVs) offer fast refueling and greater range, making them a promising option for future mobility. However, their limited refueling infrastructure and high production costs remain significant challenges that must be overcome.
